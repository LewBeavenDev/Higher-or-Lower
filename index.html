Welcome to Higher or Lower

A game by LewBeavenDev

Game Rules:

  • The aim of the game is to make the most score possible with one deck of cards
  • Bet either 'Higher' or 'Lower' as to whether or not the next card dealt, will be higher or lower than the first one dealt.
  • Increase or decrease based on how sure you are of the outcome to maximise your score
  • Ace is high
  • Game ends when you run out of score or the last card in the deck is drawn
